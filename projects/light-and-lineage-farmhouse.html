Chennai, India • Farmhouse Renovation • Heritage Retention

Light and Lineage
Farmhouse

A renovation rooted in preservation, sustainability, and memory. This project began with reverence for what was already there, creating a space that honors heritage while embracing modern comfort.

Project Details

Set within a quietly spiritual farmhouse surrounded by greenery, Light & Lineage is a renovation rooted in preservation, sustainability, and memory. Rather than starting from scratch, the design builds upon legacy. Many of the existing wooden columns were evaluated, restored, and retained, minimizing material waste while honoring their emotional and structural value.

Only what truly needed replacing was touched. New Armani marble flooring was proposed to brighten the interiors, adding clarity without overshadowing the home's spirit.

The client, deeply connected to faith and tradition, envisioned a space that felt peaceful and prayerful. This led to sacred visual moments, like the Rama Pattabhishekam mural that centers the living space and the pichwai Krishna tapestry near the dining area, each placed with purpose and devotion.

In the bedroom, a handcrafted terracotta tile ceiling was suggested not only for its warm texture, but for its natural cooling effect. Even without air conditioning, the space stays comfortable longer, reflecting how vernacular wisdom can support modern comfort.

Every design decision here is guided by quiet intention. This is a home where elegance and emotion meet, where heritage is not recreated, but revealed with care.
