Studio SARSH is an interior architecture and design studio rooted in emotional storytelling, timeless elegance, and contextual sensitivity.

Founded by Susmitha Velluru, our studio was born from a quiet but unwavering belief that spaces should do more than impress. They should make you feel seen, safe, and inspired.

After working across India and the U.S. and completing her Master's in Interior Architecture, Susmitha returned to Chennai to build a studio that felt personal, where every project is led with heart, clarity, and creative depth.
